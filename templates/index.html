Key Metrics

Recency

Days since last purchase. Customers who bought recently are more likely to respond to new offers.

Pro Tip: Lower recency = higher engagement potential

Frequency

How often purchases are made. Frequent buyers typically have higher lifetime value.

Pro Tip: More purchases = stronger customer relationship

Monetary Value

Total spend from the customer. High spenders are valuable but may be harder to retain.

Pro Tip: Combine with frequency for best insights

Purchase Patterns

Consistency and timing between purchases. Regular patterns predict future behavior.

Pro Tip: Look for consistent intervals between purchases
